Hello

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

English Fun is a website
for kids to learn and improve their English

If you want to learn English for free, you have come to the right place! Yes, it's free! We have lots of pages of high-quality video and audio, along with interactive exercises to help with spelling and pronounciation.

WHERE TO START

Explore The Site

Use the links at the top of the page to go to different sections and have a look at the materials. Try one or two pages in each section and see if the material is good for your level and what you want to learn.

Know Your Level

Beginners should start with Easy level and choose Level 1. If you have a good level of English already then try the exercises for Medium and Hard level.

Sign Up

As a registered user you can write comments and answer the discussion questions. All comments are moderated by our team so EnglishFun is a safe learning environment.

Search

Use the search feature at the top-right of every page to find the topics you want to learn more about.
